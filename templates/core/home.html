Comprehensive Internship Benefits

Why university students choose LuckyTech Innovation Ground for their industrial attachment and professional growth.

Paid Internship

Gain real-world experience while receiving financial compensation. All accepted student attachments are stipend-supported during their training.

8-Week Duration

A structured 8-week curriculum designed to fit perfectly within academic breaks, packing hands-on labs and mentor interactions.

Professional Certification

Complete practical weekend assignments to unlock a verifiable digital completion certificate that validates your technical proficiency.
